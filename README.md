## üï≥Ô∏è Levels & Gameplay

### Level 1: Limbo

A surreal, oppressive bedroom setting.

- The game begins with a disturbing act by the player character that triggers the descent.  
- Minimal interaction, heavy atmosphere, and a subtle narrative opening.

---

### Level 2: Lust

A level themed around temptation and avoidance.

- **Your goal:** Evade the manifestations of temptation that attempt to pull you in.  
- Mechanics focus on:
  - Movement  
  - Timing  
  - Resisting the easy path  

---

### Level 3: Gluttony & Greed

Two sins merged into one chaotic chase sequence.

- A massive fat demon relentlessly pursues you.  
- Your tools for survival:
  - Collect healthy food items to maintain stamina and health  
  - Avoid junk food, which drains your health
  - Outmaneuver and outrun the demon long enough to escape  

A mix of runner gameplay and resource management.

---

### Level 4: Anger

A combat-focused arena where you must fight your way past hordes of aggressive gremlins.

- Defeat enough enemies to unlock the exit and progress.  
- This level showcases:
  - The game‚Äôs shooting attack systems  
  - Enemy AI behavior  

---

### Final Level: Treachery

The final confrontation.

- A symbolic boss that represents the ultimate betrayal:
  - Of others  
  - Of yourself  
  - Of truth  
- This demon cannot be beaten. Its victory is inevitable, and its strike shatters the reality of the nightmare.

---

## üåÖ Ending: Salvation

You return to Limbo, but the bedroom is no longer dark, distorted, and oppressive.

- It is your real bedroom.  
- Everything is normal.  
- You are alive.  

You are left with the realization that the act you committed at the beginning, the one that started the descent, was wrong.

The **Inferno** was your mind's way of confronting it.
